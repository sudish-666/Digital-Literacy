# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Online Safety

1. **Use strong, unique passwords** for every account 
   and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever 
   possible.

2. **Never click on suspicious links** received via 
   SMS, WhatsApp, or email — even if they appear to 
   come from known contacts or banks.

3. **Verify before you trust** – Always verify the 
   identity of anyone contacting you online, especially 
   for buying/selling transactions.

4. **Keep your devices updated** – Regularly update 
   your phone's OS and apps to patch known security 
   vulnerabilities.

5. **Avoid using public Wi-Fi** for banking, UPI 
   transactions, or logging into sensitive accounts.

6. **Review app permissions** – Do not grant unnecessary 
   permissions (camera, contacts, location) to apps 
   you download.

## UPI & Financial Safety (Mandatory Tips)

7. **Your UPI PIN is only for sending money** – You 
   NEVER need to enter your UPI PIN or scan a QR code 
   to RECEIVE money. If someone asks you to do this, 
   it is a fraud.

8. **Never share OTP, CVV, or UPI PIN** with anyone — 
   not even someone claiming to be from your bank, 
   Google Pay or PhonePe support.

## Reporting Cybercrime

If you or someone you know becomes a victim of 
any cybercrime:

-  Report online: **cybercrime.gov.in**
-  Call helpline: **1930** (available 24x7)

Act immediately — the faster you report, the higher 
the chance of freezing the fraudulent transaction.
